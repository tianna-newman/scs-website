About Specialist Contracting Services

Specialist Contracting Services is led by Michael, a hands-on surface preparation specialist who has spent decades working on concrete floors across Western Australia.

Michael Grave

Founder & Surface Preparation Specialist
46+ years of surface prep experience

Michael is the face behind Specialist Contracting Services, bringing over 46 years of hands-on experience in concrete surface preparation. Throughout his career he has specialised in shot blasting and surface grinding for industrial, commercial and residential projects across Western Australia. Known for his practical problem-solving, attention to detail and commitment to doing the job right the first time, Michael takes pride in preparing every surface to the standard needed for long-lasting coatings and finishes.
