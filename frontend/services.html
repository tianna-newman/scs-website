Shot Blasting

Shot blasting uses high-velocity steel shot to mechanically remove contaminants, old coatings, and laitance from concrete surfaces. This process creates an ideal surface profile for coating adhesion while being dust-controlled.

Surface Grinding

Surface grinding uses diamond-embedded tools to level uneven concrete, remove surface defects, and achieve a smooth, uniform finish. It's essential for preparing floors for epoxy, polished concrete, or other specialized coatings.
