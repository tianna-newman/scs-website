Why Do We Need the Surface Preparation?

Surface preparation is the foundation of any successful coating, painting, or flooring project. It involves cleaning, profiling, and treating concrete or other surfaces to ensure optimal adhesion for coatings, sealers, and overlays. Without proper surface preparation, coatings can fail prematurely, leading to costly repairs and downtime. At SCS, we use industry-leading equipment and techniques to deliver consistent, reliable results every time.

Our Services

Captive Shot Blasting

Shot blasting uses high-velocity steel shot to mechanically remove contaminants, old coatings, and laitance from concrete surfaces. This process creates an ideal surface profile for coating adhesion while being dust-controlled.

Surface Grinding

Surface grinding uses diamond-embedded tools to level uneven concrete, remove surface defects, and achieve a smooth, uniform finish. It's essential for preparing floors for epoxy, polished concrete, or other specialized coatings.
